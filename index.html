Surroundings and Facilities

The resort Tále pod Chopkom is one of the most beautiful and the most appropriate spots for spending pleasant moments of active rest. Fascinating healthy forests in this area can persuade anybody. Our 4 apartments are located exactly there. They are placed immediately next to each other in a separate wing of the building on the 4th floor with a beautiful view on the slopes of the surrounding ski resorts.
